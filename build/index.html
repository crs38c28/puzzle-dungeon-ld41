BASIC RULES
This is a match-three (Tile-matching) puzzle game.
Stage size is 6 x 11, and you can't stay in the red cross area(Toppest area)
Each time you clear one line, you can get powers.
match-three: get 1 power.
match-four: get 2 powers......
There are 4 kinds of powers.
Once your character get enough powers. He will cast a spell to attack BOSS or buff/heal himself automatically.
BOSS will attack after you drop blocks several times.(There is a CD timer.)
When BOSS at low HP, it will use special ULTIMATE SKILLS to harass you.
COMBO SYSTEM
Here is a combo(chain) system.
You will get more powers when you use combo.
CONTROL
: Rotate
: Move Left
: Move Right
: Drop
